Check my numbers

How would your numbers have done across every draw on record?

⚠ Just for fun — not gambling or financial advice. Past draws don't predict future ones; every combination is equally unlikely on any given draw. The lottery is a negative-expected-value bet — play for entertainment only.

We scan the full draw history we hold for the game you pick and count how many of your numbers appear in each past draw (plus the bonus ball, where there is one). Digit games are checked for an exact "straight" match and an any-order "box" match. Depth varies by game, and recent-window games keep only a rolling history, so older draws may not be included.
