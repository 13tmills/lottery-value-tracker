Head-to-head history

Every meeting on record (2020 onward, including playoffs). The chart shows the final margin from Team A's point of view — bars above the line are wins.

Who's hot, who's cold

Skill-position players whose average fantasy points over their last three games ran at least 12% above (hot) or below (cold) their season average — a simple read on who finished the season trending up or down. Fantasy points use standard PPR-style scoring as a single performance index.

Player performance by opponent

Type a player to see how they've performed against each opponent over their career (per-game fantasy points and yards). Splits built on fewer than four games are marked as small samples — read them with caution.
